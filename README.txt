Protocol: smallcellRNA
Type: Protocol
Title: Expression profiling for low input materials
Version: 06202013
Date: 2015-06-15
Author: Sheng Liu
Maintainer: Sheng Liu <shengl@interchange.ubc.ca>
Description: 
smallcellRNA is designed for profiling of expression of “rare” cell populations in vivo at locus and genome-wide scale. It includes protocols for RNA extraxtion - reverse transcription - qPCR/library for low input materials.
